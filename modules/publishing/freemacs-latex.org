* AUCTeX

Use [[https://www.gnu.org/software/auctex/][AUCTeX]] for writing documents using \LaTeX{}.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp :tangle yes :noweb yes
  (use-package auctex
    :straight t
    :defer t
    :custom
    (TeX-auto-save t)
    (TeX-parse-self t)
    (TeX-master nil)
    <<auctex_custom>>)
#+end_src
